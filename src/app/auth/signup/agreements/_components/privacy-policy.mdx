<h1 id="cookie-policy">3. Cookie Policy</h1>

### About Cookies

A “cookie” is a small text file stored on your computer, tablet or phone when you visit a website. Some cookies are deleted when you close your browser; these are known as session cookies. Others remain on your device until they expire, or you delete them from your cache; these are persistent cookies and enable us to remember things about you as a returning visitor. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit [All About Cookies](http://www.allaboutcookies.org/).

We use both SESSION COOKIES (which expire once you close your web browser) and PERSISTENT COOKIES (which stay on your device until you delete them).

We have also grouped our cookies into the following categories to make it easier for you to understand why we need them:

1. **Functionality:** These cookies enable the technical performance of our websites and allow us to “remember” the choices you make and your preferences.

2. **Performance/Analytical:** These cookies allow us to collect certain information about how you navigate the Sites. They help us understand which parts of our websites are interesting to you and which are not and what we can do to improve them.

<h1 id="how-we-use-cookies">4. How we use cookies</h1>
